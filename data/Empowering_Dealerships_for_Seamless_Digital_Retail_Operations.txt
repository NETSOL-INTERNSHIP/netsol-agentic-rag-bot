Empowering Dealerships for Seamless Digital Retail Operations
Empowering Dealerships for Seamless Digital Retail Operations
In this panel discussion hosted by NETSOL Technologies, Eva Kellershof, Vice President of Sales for North America and Europe, joins Steve Hall, Senior Consultant at NCM Associates, to explore how dealerships can build truly seamless
digital retail automotive
operations, moving beyond simply deploying tools toward creating connected, efficient, and staff-supported processes that drive measurable outcomes for sales teams, customers, and OEM partners.
About this webinar
The adoption of digital retail technology in automotive dealerships has accelerated significantly, but many dealerships are finding that deploying platforms is the easy part. The harder challenge is operationalizing them: getting sales teams to use them consistently, connecting them cleanly to existing DMS and
automotive finance software
workflows, and building the feedback loops that allow continuous improvement over time.
This session addresses that operational challenge directly, bringing together NETSOL's retail platform expertise and NCM Associates' deep dealership consulting experience to give dealerships a practical framework for making digital retail work in practice, not just in theory.
What you'll learn
This session is built for dealership principals, general managers, digital retail managers, and OEM field teams responsible for rolling out and optimising digital retail operations. It covers:
How to streamline dealership operations and automate workflows to reduce manual overhead and speed up deal cycles.
What effective staff training and support programmes look like for building genuine digital retail capability across sales teams.
How analytics and performance data drive better decisions about customer behaviour, conversion rates, and process bottlenecks.
Why customer feedback loops are essential for continuous optimisation of digital retail products and services.
How to build cohesive collaboration between online and in-store teams to deliver a consistent customer experience.
Key themes from the discussion
Three operational priorities emerged from the panel as the foundation for successful digital retail implementation:
Dealer alignment before deployment.
The dealerships achieving the best results from digital retail are those that map their existing workflows before selecting or configuring their platform, not after. Steve Hall's experience across hundreds of NCM Associates clients consistently points to this as the single biggest differentiator between successful and struggling implementations.
Analytics must drive decisions, not just report them.
Most dealerships now have access to more performance data than ever, but value is only realised when that data connects to specific operational actions. The session covers how to build data review cadences that turn analytics into decisions on staffing, process design, and customer journey improvements.
Staff enablement is ongoing, not a one-time event.
Digital retail tools only perform at their potential when the people using them are confident and supported.
Smarter dealership operations
examines the broader operational and competitive roadmap dealerships must follow, including how leading dealers are structuring continuous training to sustain performance even as staff changes over time.
