NETSOL is proud to announce our participation as a Silver Sponsor in this year’s AFC Autumn Conference, set to take place on November 25, 2025, in London. As the asset finance world faces seismic shifts in regulation, technology and business models, there has never been a more critical moment to engage, debate and shape the future of this industry. Our team of eight senior delegates will be on the ground for a day of rich discussion and forward looking insights.
Engaging debate: The role of intermediaries in SME lending
One of the highlights of this year’s conference is a spirited debate taking place from 5:00 pm to 5:30 pm during the event: “This House believes the future of SME lending will be direct and not via intermediaries.”
We are especially excited to announce that our Sales Director for Europe Jason Hurwitz will be taking part in this debate, representing our vision for a more efficient, transparent and technology-driven lending model. He will argue that the
future of SME
(small and medium enterprise) finance lies in direct relationships powered by digital platforms, rather than relying on traditional broker intermediaries.
Jason’s participation underscores NETSOL’s deeply held belief: in a world where asset finance is powered by real-time data, AI-enabled risk assessment and seamless customer journeys, intermediaries’ role must evolve and direct lending is the most scalable, responsible path forward. Be sure to catch this session; it promises to be one of the most thought-provoking ones of the day.

Why the AFC Autumn Conference matters for NETSOL
This year’s conference comes at a pivotal moment for the asset finance sector. With regulatory pressures mounting, customer expectations evolving and technology reshaping business models, asset finance leaders must navigate change proactively. For NETSOL, attending AFC is not just about listening, it’s about joining the conversation, contributing our perspective and co-creating the future.
This conference represents a unique opportunity for NETSOL to connect with industry leaders, regulators and innovators under one roof. With the asset finance landscape rapidly evolving, it’s critical to exchange ideas, explore emerging trends and identify practical solutions that drive efficiency and growth.
For NETSOL, the conference is not just an event; it’s a strategic opportunity to shape the future of asset finance and reinforce our position as a trusted partner for financial institutions in Europe and worldwide.
