Keynote spotlight: “Beyond LLMs, where is the next competitive advantage?”
One of the most engaging and thought-provoking moments at the summit came on Day Two when Dario Morelli, Vice President – AI & Data Science at NETSOL, took the stage to deliver his keynote titled “Beyond LLMs: Where Is the Next Competitive Advantage?” Dario explored the limitations of today’s large language models (LLMs) and urged the audience to look beyond these technologies to uncover the next frontier of AI innovation.
Dario expands on these themes in the
AI best practices and future of AI
episode of the Transcend AI Labs podcast, covering how organizations can move from LLM experimentation to production-grade AI systems built on domain knowledge, causal reasoning, and responsible deployment frameworks.
His insights were a call to action for businesses and tech leaders to think strategically about the future of AI and its potential to unlock new competitive advantages which challenged the audience to think beyond the hype of large language models (LLMs). Dario highlighted:
1. Recognizing the limits of today’s LLM infrastructure
Marginal gains from newer models:
Even with breakthroughs like GPT-5, improvements remain incremental.
Reliability concerns:
AI agents built on LLMs inherit these limitations, making large-scale production deployments challenging.
2. Three strategic elements for the next edge
Product Mindset
Move beyond experiments; embed AI as monetizable products focusing on revenue growth, cost efficiency, risk management, and customer experience. Product managers must bridge customer needs and AI capabilities.
Knowledge > Data
The real advantage lies in turning raw data into structured knowledge. Techniques such as knowledge graphs, ontologies, and causal relationships transform proprietary data into context-rich insights and create defensible competitive advantages.
Self-Awareness
Organizations must address three types of “debt”:
Technical:
Start from the existing tech stack; avoid chasing shiny tools.
Strategic:
Align initiatives under a clear direction to avoid fragmentation.
Cultural:
Foster experimentation and a learning mindset to continuously adapt.

NETSOL products showcased at the event
In addition to the keynote, NETSOL showcased its
generative ai consulting
services, which are transforming the asset finance industry. The following products were highlighted at the event:
Flow AI
Flow AI, a cutting-edge AI-powered solution, automates and enhances financial document handling in credit and funding applications. By utilizing AI-driven data extraction, comparison, and discrepancy detection, Flow AI streamlines the approval process and integrates seamlessly into existing workflows, improving accuracy and compliance in credit decisioning. The solution ensures the efficient management of documents, such as tax returns and bank statements, saving time and reducing manual errors in the underwriting process
Check AI
Check AI is an AI credit analyst assistant designed to optimize the credit evaluation process by integrating multiple data sources and generating comprehensive reports. These reports provide SWOT analysis, asset insights, and applicant insights, making credit decisions faster and more accurate. Check AI’s modular design allows it to seamlessly integrate with existing workflows and is designed to automate document processing while providing data-backed insights. By automating repetitive tasks, Check AI empowers credit analysts to focus on high-value decisions
Transcend AI Labs
Transcend AI Labs represents NETSOL’s commitment to transforming business operations through AL and ML services innovation. This product focuses on advanced AI services tailored to meet the challenges of industries ranging from finance, banking, fintech, to insurance, and auto finance. The lab’s capabilities extend beyond LLMs, using causal AI and knowledge engineering to deliver AI solutions that drive sustainable growth, enhance efficiency, and provide personalized experiences for customers.
